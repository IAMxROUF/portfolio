02 — About Me

I'm Abdur Rouf Ahmed, an Experience Designer and UX Researcher pursuing my M.Des in Experience Design at NIFT Mumbai. With 5+ internships spanning UI/UX, XR design, product design, service design, and design research, I bring an end-to-end perspective — from user research and wireframing through high-fidelity prototyping and usability testing.

I believe the most powerful design happens where tradition meets technology. My work transforms traditional practices into immersive digital experiences — creating human-centered solutions that serve society while unlocking tangible market value for businesses and communities.
